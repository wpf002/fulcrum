# Buyer tool — terms & consent disclosure

**Version:** 2026-07-buyer-v1
**DRAFT — pending review by licensed counsel. Not legal advice.**

## What this tool does

You are using a home-affordability and buyer-readiness tool provided on behalf
of a licensed real estate agent (the "Agent"). The tool estimates your buying
power from figures you enter and, if you choose to share your contact details,
sends them to that Agent so they can help you find a home.

## What we collect

- Figures you enter (income, monthly debts, down payment, interest rate) and
  the resulting affordability estimate.
- Your buying preferences (price range, target area, bedrooms, timeline).
- Your readiness answers (e.g. whether you are pre-approved).
- The contact details you provide (name, email, and phone if you enter one).
- Technical details needed to prove this consent: the time, your IP address,
  and your browser's user-agent string.

We do **not** pull your credit file, and we do **not** access your bank
accounts or financial institutions.

## Who receives it

Your information goes to the **Agent whose tool you are using**. It is not
sold, and it is not shared with other agents.

## How we contact you

You choose each channel separately. Nothing is pre-checked and nothing is
bundled:

- **Email** — you may receive matching homes and updates from the Agent.
- **SMS** — only if you check the SMS box. Message and data rates may apply.
  Reply STOP to stop.
- **Automated calls/texts (TCPA)** — only if you check that box, you expressly
  consent to receive calls and/or text messages from the Agent at the number
  you provided **using an automatic telephone dialing system and/or an
  artificial or prerecorded voice**. This consent is **not a condition of
  purchasing any goods or services.** You may revoke it at any time.

You can withdraw any of these consents at any time by replying STOP (SMS),
using the unsubscribe link (email), or contacting the Agent.

## Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the information you
submitted by contacting the Agent. Depending on where you live, you may have
additional rights under state privacy law.

## Changes

If these terms change, we publish a new version. The version and a hash of the
exact text you were shown are stored with your consent record, so what you
agreed to can always be reproduced.
